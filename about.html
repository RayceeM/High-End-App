About us

With the constant increase of mobile people who go around with both their personal and working lives, catering to their meal needs and still maintaining high quality in food and services is getting more difficult each day. High End App is a mobile app built by Racheal Mwatela as part of her final project as an undergraduate. With the help of some of her friends,High End app was launched.We have been in contact with many businesses investors who are looking forward to be part of the team. Currently High end app covers 3 major cities in Kenya that is Nairobi,Kisumu and Mombasa with a vision of expanding in future.
Taking into consideration the rapid growth and uptake of internet services we foresee a brighter future for this application as it is convient.

Vision

To provide an online platform where eateries can provide convinent services and interact with their customers.

Mission

To provide high end services and quality meals.
